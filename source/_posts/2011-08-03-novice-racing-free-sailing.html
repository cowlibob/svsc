Club members are again invited to come and sail on 6th August at 1pm.

These sessions are aimed at any member new or old, whether boat owner or not, to come and try your sailing skills in a non-competitive environment. For more information or to let me know you can come or if you are interested but can’t come this month contact Ava at novice-sailing@sheffieldviking.org.uk
